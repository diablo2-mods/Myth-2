Final Uber Quest
*To activate the quest, you need to collect 9 keys of three types on Hell Difficulty.
*Keys can be bought in Harrogate or knocked out of certain monsters.
*Key of Terror - the Countess drops it in the dungeons below the Forgotten Tower.
*Key of Hate - the Summoner drops it in the Arcane Sanctuary.
*Key of Destruction - Nihlathak drops it in his temple.
*Weld 3 different keys in the cube while in Harrogath on Hell Difficulty.
*And a portal will be opened to one of the 3 uber levels.
