Goblins
 
*Kill them before they disappear in their portals.
 
 
Treasure Bandit
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
You can meet it on Nightmare and above.
The chance to meet it in: Part 1 - 5%, Part 2 - 6%, Part 3 - 7%
Drops on Nightmare: Items (Normal, Exeptional and some Elite) + Gems, Runes and Bags with Gold
Drops on Hell: Items (Normal, Exeptional and Elite) + Gems, Runes and Bags with Gold
Drops on Torment: Items (Normal, Exeptional, Elite and Legendary) + Gems, Runes and Bags with Gold
Drops on Inferno: Items (Legendary), Jewels and Charms + Gems, Runes, Broken Seals and Bags with Gold
Locations in Act 1: Blood Moor, Cold Plains, Burial Grounds, Stony Field, Tristram, Dark Wood, Black Marsh, Tamoe Highland,
Monastery Gate, Outer Cloister, Inner Cloister, Cathedral, Catacombs Level 1, Moo Moo Farm
Locations in Act 2: Rocky Waste, Dry Hills, Far Oasis, Lost City, Valley of Snakes, Canyon of the Magi,
Palace Cellar Level 2, Maggot Lair Level 1, Tal Rasha's Tomb
Locations in Act 3: Spider Forest, Great Marsh, Flayer Jungle, Lower Kurast, Kurast Bazaar, Upper Kurast, Travincal
Locations in Act 4: Outer Steppes
Locations in Act 5: Bloody Foothills, Rigid Highlands, Arreat Plateau, Frozen Tundra, Throne of Destruction
+ All the Levels of Gilded Baron and Gelatinous Sire (on Nightmare and Hell Only)
 
Gem Hoarder
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
You can meet it on Nightmare and above.
The chance to meet it in: Part 1 - 3%, Part 2 - 4%, Part 3 - 5%
Drops on Nightmare: Chipped and Flawed Gems
Drops on Hell: Chipped, Flawed and Normal Gems
Drops on Torment: Chipped, Flawed, Normal and Flawless Gems
Drops on Inferno: All Gems
Locations in Act 1: Den of Evil, Cave Level 1-2, Hole Level 1-2, Tower Cellar Level 1, Jail Level 2, Catacombs Level 2
Locations in Act 2: Harem Level 2, Stony Tomb Level 1-2, Maggot Lair Level 2, Claw Viper Temple Level 1, Tal Rasha's Tomb, Arcane Sanctuary
Locations in Act 3: Kurast Causeway, Resident Evil Level 1, Flayer Dungeon Level 1,
Disused Fane, Forgotten Temple, Disused Reliquary, Durance of Hate Level 1
Locations in Act 4: City of the Damned
Locations in Act 5: Crystalline Passage, Glacial Trail, The Ancients' Way, The Worldstone Keep Level 2
 
Odious Collector
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
You can meet it on Nightmare and above.
The chance to meet it in: Part 1 - 3%, Part 2 - 4%, Part 3 - 5%
Drops on Nightmare: Runes (Low Levels) and Heroic Potions
Drops on Hell: Runes (Low and Medium Levels) and Heroic Potions
Drops on Torment: Runes (All Levels), Heroic Potions and Great Stones (Green)
Drops on Inferno: Runes / Broken Seals (All Levels), Heroic Potions, Great Stones (Green / Blue) and Tarot Cards
Locations in Act 1: Crypt, Mausoleum, Tower Cellar Level 2-4, Jail Level 1 and 3, Catacombs Level 3
Locations in Act 2: Sewers Level 1-3, Palace Cellar Level 1, Halls of the Dead Level 1, Maggot Lair Level 3, Ancient Tunnels, Tal Rasha's Tomb
Locations in Act 3: Spider Cave, Spider Cavern, Resident Evil Level 2, Flayer Dungeon Level 2,
Forgotten Reliquary, Ruined Fane, Durance of Hate Level 2
Locations in Act 4: Plains of Despair
Locations in Act 5: Frozen River, Icy Cellar, Halls of Anguish, Halls of Death's Calling, The Worldstone Keep Level 1
 
Gilded Baron
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
You can meet it on Torment 1 and above.
The chance to meet it in: Part 2 - 3%, Part 3 - 4%
Drops on Torment: Gold (~110 to 175k)
Drops on Inferno: Gold (~145 to 225k)
Locations in Act 1: Underground Passage Level 1-2, Pit Level 1-2
Locations in Act 2: Halls of the Dead Level 2, Tal Rasha's Tomb
Locations in Act 3: Resident Evil Level 3, Flayer Dungeon Level 3, Sewers Level 1
Locations in Act 4: River of Flame
Locations in Act 5: Abaddon in Frigid Highlands, Pit of Acheron in Arreat Plateau, Infernal Pit
 
Gelatinous Sire
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
You can meet it on Torment 1 and above.
Upon death it splits into 3 gelatinous spawns, which again splits into 3 when finally killed.
The chance to meet it in: Part 2 - 3%, Part 3 - 4%
Drops on Torment: Items (Normal, Exeptional, Elite and Legendary) + Gems, Runes and Bags with Gold
Drops on Inferno: Items (Legendary), Jewels and Charms + Gems, Runes, Broken Seals and Bags with Gold
Locations in Act 1: Barracks, Catacombs Level 4
Locations in Act 2: Palace Cellar Level 3, Halls of the Dead Level 3, Tal Rasha's Tomb
Locations in Act 3: Ruined Temple, Durance of Hate Level 3
Locations in Act 4: Chaos Sanctum
Locations in Act 5: Halls of Vaught, Ice Prison, The Worldstone Keep Level 3
 
Blood Thief
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
It can appear after the death of a boss or superunique monster on Inferno 1 and above.
The chance to meet it in: Part 3 - 1%
Drops on Inferno: Runes, Broken Seals and Blood Shards
 
 
Notes:
*Treasure Bandit and Gelatinous Spawns can drop Magic, Rare, Set and Unique Items (Weapons, All Armor, Rings, Amulets, Jewels and Charms).
*Only Blood Thief can be spawned in The Arreat Summit, Matron's Den, The Forgotten Sands, Furnace of Pain and Uber Tristram.
