Break through hordes of enemies and kill super unique monsters and bosses to get shards for creating Challenge Trophies.
The further you go, the stronger the monsters and the higher the drop chance of Trophy Shards.
 
 
Rules:
Daytime or Nighttime.
No waypoints for levels.
Five acts and five shards for one reward.
Trophy Shards can drop from all Super Unique Monsters and Bosses.
The Cow King, Diablo Clone and Uber Bosses in Uber Tristram can drop all the five shards.
Act Bosses and Uber Bosses in Uber Tristram have the highest drop chance of shards.
 
 
Cube Recipe:
All Trophy Shards (1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5) = Challenge Trophy
 
*Active in Charm Inventory.
