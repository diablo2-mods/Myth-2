- Widescreen resolution, 1068x600 by default.
- Auto gold pickup.
- By holding CTRL + LEFT MOUSE BUTTON, you can move items to the inventory, chest and cube.
- A refresh button in Gamble to change items without closing the tab.

- Sockets are possible in: Gloves = 2, Belts = 2, Boots = 2, Throwing Weapons = 3, Quivers = 1.
