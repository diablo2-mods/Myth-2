MYTH
Part III: Dawn of the Black Sun
 
Fanatics of the Black Sun cult broke the Seals of Darkness and scattered their shards across Sanctuary.
Shortly thereafter, the creatures of evil found the shards of Seals, became stronger and summoned powerful minions.
Kill them all and recreate the Seals of Darkness to gain their power.
 
General features:
*New palettes for all Acts.
*Resistances to Fire, Cold, Lighting and Poison on Difficulties: Inferno 1 = -200, Inferno 2 = -225, Inferno 3 = -250.
*Static Field: 70%.
*The chance of critical hit for Super Unique Monsters and all Bosses = 10%.
*The level of monsters on Difficulties: Inferno 1 = 140, Inferno 2 = 150, Inferno 3 = 160.
*The level of super unique monsters, act and uber bosses on Difficulties: Inferno 1 = 160, Inferno 2 = 170, Inferno 3 = 180.
*Elemental damage for all monsters: Inferno 1, 2 and 3 = +30.
*Bonus to monster skill level: Inferno 1 = +12, Inferno 2 = +14, Inferno 3 = +16.
*The super unique monsters and bosses have new powerful minions.
*Part 2 item drops are the same as on Torment 3 Difficulty.
*The drop chance of the Legendary Items (L): Inferno 1 = 1 in 40, Inferno 2 = 1 in 30, Inferno 3 = 1 in 20.
*Maximum number of sockets in items when dropping on Difficulties: Inferno 1 = 3, Inferno 2 = 4, Inferno 3 = 6

*Fanatics can drop Blood Shards.
*The higher the difficulty, the higher the drop chance.
 
From ordinary monsters: Inferno 1 = 1 in 4000, Inferno 2 = 1 in 2000, Inferno 3 = 1 in 1000
From champions: Inferno 1 = 1 in 500, Inferno 2 = 1 in 333, Inferno 3 = 1 in 200
From unique monsters: Inferno 1 = 1 in 300, Inferno 2 = 1 in 200, Inferno 3 = 1 in 100
 
*Blood Goblins can drop Blood Shards, too.

Seals of Darkness
*Required Level: 85
 
*Collect 2 required pieces of a Seal and place them along with 2 Blood Shards in the Cube to create a Seal of Darkness.
*Or find a Broken Seal and place it along with 5 Blood Shards in the Cube to create a Seal of Darkness.
*Place 2 the same Seals of Darkness and 1 Blood Shard in the Cube to create the next higher Seal of Darkness.
 
*All stats of the Seals can be found in
Gems, Runes...
 
*All words with the Seals can be found in
Words
 
*The drop chance of Seal Shards from certain monsters and bosses on Difficulties: Inferno 1 = 1 in 300, Inferno 2 = 1 in 100, Inferno 3 = 1 in 25.
*The drop chance of Seals of Darkness from certain monsters and bosses on Difficulties: Inferno 1 = 1 in 6000, Inferno 2 = 1 in 2000, Inferno 3 = 1 in 500.
*You will also find Broken Seals when killing different monsters (BS vs Runes - 1 in 10).
