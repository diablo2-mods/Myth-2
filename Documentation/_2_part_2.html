MYTH
Part II: The Curse of the Damned
 
With the help of Legendary Items, the servants of evil have become stronger than ever.
Take possession of Legendary Items to become stronger than them and challenge the strongest of them.
 
General features:
*New palettes for all Acts.
*Resistances to Fire, Cold, Lighting and Poison on Difficulties: Torment 1 = -125, Torment 2 = -150, Torment 3 = -175.
*Static Field: 60%.
*The chance of critical hit for Super Unique Monsters and all Bosses = 8%.
*The level of monsters on Difficulties: Torment 1 = 110, Torment 2 = 120, Torment 3 = 130.
*The level of super unique monsters, act and uber bosses on Difficulties: Torment 1 = 130, Torment 2 = 140, Torment 3 = 150.
*Elemental damage for all monsters: Torment 1 = +10, Torment 2 = +20, Torment 3 = +30.
*Bonus to monster skill level: Torment 1 = +8, Torment 2 = +9, Torment 3 = +10.
*Many super unique monsters and bosses have new AI, abilities and skills.
*Part 1 item drops are the same as on Hell Difficulty.
*Maximum number of sockets in items when dropping on Difficulties: Torment 1 = 3, Torment 2 = 4, Torment 3 = 6

Items of the Legendary Type
 
*Weapons, armor, rings, amulets, arrows and bolts (including improved versions of old set and unique items) of the Legendary Type can drop on Torment 1 Difficulty and above.
*The maximum number of sockets for gloves and boots = 4, for shields = 6, for quivers = 3, for throwing axes = 6, for throwing spears = 4.
*Set and unique throwing weapons, arrows and bolts restore most of the ammo in the Cube, without any reagents.
 
*The drop chance (relative to Normal, Exceptional and Elite): Torment 1 = 1 in 70, Torment 2 = 1 in 60, Torment 3 = 1 in 50.
 
 
Ramaladni's Gift
 
*Ramaladni's Gift can only be applied to a weapon or armor that does not have a socket.
*When used, it adds 1-3 sockets to Armor, Shield and One-Handed Weapon (1-4 sockets to Two-Handed Weapon).
*It can drop from any monsters and chests.
 
*The drop chance (relative to Gems / Runes / Jewels)
1 in 650
